YOU BETTER NOT

This is no joke. We are over 1,000,000 on the Scoville Scale. That tis almost 100x hotter than a jalapeño. You better pack a lunch cuz this one packs a punch! Lips on fire is a common side effect.

HOT BUFFalo

This blend of spice and peppers is anchored by habanero, a little vinegar for tang. It's spicy but not "slap yo mama" spicy. Still pretty damn hot. No for wussies! Lips will tingle for about 3 minutes.

YOU WON'T

This is probably more what you are use to. Cayenne, jalapeño, crushed red chili, vinegar for tang and some butter to cut the heat (a little). This will leave you wanting a nice frothy beverage with very little lingering sting.

SPICY GARLIC

Cayenne pepper, jalapeño and paprika with fresh ground garlic, tangy vinegar and smooth buttery flavor. Don't be chicken to try this one. Is has depth and it packs a nice punch!

HONEY HOT
